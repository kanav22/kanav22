# Speaking & Talk Proposals

Conference and meetup submissions aligned with my platform engineering work.

---

## Proposal 1: MVI at Scale in Jetpack Compose

**Audience:** Senior Android engineers, tech leads  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Abstract:** How unidirectional data flow prevents state bugs as Compose codebases grow past 50 screens. Real patterns from fintech delivery and open-source reference apps—including testing ViewModels without Robolectric hacks.

**Key takeaways:**
- When MVI beats MVVM (and when it doesn't)
- Structuring `UiState` for Compose performance
- CI gates that enforce architectural boundaries

---

## Proposal 2: Offline-First Mobile in Regulated Domains

**Audience:** Mobile + backend engineers, architects  
**Duration:** 40 minutes  
**Abstract:** Payments and banking apps cannot afford blank screens on flaky networks. A practical walkthrough of cache-first repositories, SQLDelight/Room tradeoffs, and sync conflict strategies that satisfy compliance reviewers.

---

## Proposal 3: Bootstrapping Android Platform Teams with Open Source Standards

**Audience:** Engineering managers, principal engineers  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Abstract:** How to use internal (or public) platform starters—multi-module Gradle, Detekt, Macrobenchmark, golden tests—to raise the floor for every squad without centralising all development.

**Demo repo:** [android-platform-starter](https://github.com/kanav22/android-platform-starter)
